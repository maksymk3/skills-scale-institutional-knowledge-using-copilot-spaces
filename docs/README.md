# OctoAcme Project Management Process Docs

## Overview

OctoAcme operates on a structured lifecycle-driven approach to project management that emphasizes **customer-first principles**, **iterative delivery**, and **clear ownership**. The organization follows five core phases: Initiation (validating business need and aligning stakeholders through a Project One-pager), Planning (breaking work into shippable increments with prioritized backlogs and acceptance criteria), Execution (daily standups and sprint-based delivery with rigorous PR workflows), Release (standardized deployment with pre-release requirements and rollback plans), and Close & Retrospective (capturing learnings for continuous improvement).

Key to OctoAcme's success are three primary personas working in close coordination: **Project Managers** who coordinate delivery, manage schedules, risks, and communications; **Product Managers** who define outcomes, prioritize backlogs, and measure success; and **Developers** who implement features, collaborate on design, and maintain quality standards. This clear role definition prevents overlap and ensures accountability.

Project governance is reinforced through a formal escalation hierarchy—team-level issues are triaged in daily standups, then escalated to PM, Product Lead, and ultimately to Sponsor level if needed—and a consistent communication cadence of daily standups, weekly PM/PdM syncs, and monthly stakeholder updates. Quality and delivery rigor are embedded throughout execution via disciplined PR workflows, comprehensive testing strategies (unit, integration, and end-to-end), and security scanning. Risk management is formalized through a Risk Register updated weekly, with each risk assigned an ID, description, impact/likelihood assessment, owner, and mitigation plan. Finally, retrospectives held after each sprint or significant milestone ensure continuous improvement through action items with clear owners and due dates.

## How to Use These Docs

- **New team members**: Start with the [Project Management Overview](./octoacme-project-management-overview.md) for context, then dive into specific phases as your project progresses.
- **Starting a new project**: Follow the [Project Initiation Guide](./octoacme-project-initiation.md) to get aligned on goals and stakeholders.
- **During execution**: Refer to [Execution & Tracking](./octoacme-execution-and-tracking.md) and [Risk Management & Communication](./octoacme-risks-and-communication.md) for day-to-day guidance.
- **Before release**: Use the [Release & Deployment Guide](./octoacme-release-and-deployment.md) checklist.
- **After milestones or sprints**: Review [Retrospective & Continuous Improvement](./octoacme-retrospective-and-continuous-improvement.md) to capture learnings.
